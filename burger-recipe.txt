INGREDIENTS 
BURGER
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon mustard (regular or Dijon)
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 small onion, finely grated
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 lb medium ground beef

DIRECTIONS
1. Lightly oil grill & heat BBQ to medium.
2. Whisk egg in a bowl & add next 6 ingredients.
3. Crumble in beef & using your hands or a fork, gently mix together.
4. Gently shape (don’t firmly press) mixture into burgers about ¾ inch thick.
5. Using your thumb, make a shallow depression in the centre of each burger to prevent puffing up during cooking.
6. Place burgers on the grill, close lid & BBQ until NO LONGER PINK INSIDE, turning once, about 6 – 8 minutes per side.
7. Place inside a warm crusty bun & add your favourite toppings.
